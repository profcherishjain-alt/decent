Hamare Baare Mein

Kota ki Shaan,
Bachchon ka Abhimaan

2005 mein sthaapit, Decent School Kota aaj Rajasthan ke shreshtha schools mein gina jaata hai. Hamara mission hai – har bachche ki chhipi hui pratibha ko nikhaarna aur use ek zimmedaar nagarik banana.

Hamare shikshak sirf paath-pustak nahi padhate, balki jeevan ki seekh bhi dete hain. Hamara campus 5 acre mein failaa hua hai, jis mein library, auditorium, aur indoor stadium bhi hai.
